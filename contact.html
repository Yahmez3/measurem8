Contact us

Spotted a wonky conversion? Want a unit we don't have yet? We'd genuinely like to hear about it.

MeasureMate is a small independent site, and reader emails are how it gets better. Three kinds of mail are especially welcome:

  • Corrections. A conversion or ingredient density that looks wrong. Please include the conversion you tried (for example, "2 cups of honey to grams") and the result you expected — that lets us check the exact factor involved.
  • Suggestions. A unit, ingredient or quick-reference table you'd find useful that we don't cover yet.
  • Questions. Anything about how the converter works or how a conversion is calculated. It's worth checking the FAQ first — there's a fair chance your question is already answered there.

The best way to reach us is email:

hello@measurem8.app

We read everything and aim to reply within a few days. There's no support team behind the curtain — just the people who build the site — so thanks in advance for your patience.
