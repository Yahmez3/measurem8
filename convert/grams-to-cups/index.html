How to convert grams to cups

To convert grams to cups, divide the grams by the ingredient’s grams-per-cup density (e.g. 250 g of flour ÷ 125 ≈ 2 cups).

FAQ

How many cups is 100 grams?

For flour, 100 g ≈ 0.8 cups; for sugar, 100 g ≈ 0.5 cups. It varies by ingredient.

Why does grams-to-cups change by ingredient?

Because density differs — the same weight takes up different volume depending on the ingredient.
